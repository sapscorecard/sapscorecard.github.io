About this site

350 Spokane is a volunteer-run nonprofit building an effective movement for climate action in the greater Spokane area, promoting a just transition to 100% fossil-free energy and a low-carbon economy that works for everyone. This site is one of the ways in which 350 Spokane is helping keep Spokane's people and its leaders informed and aware of the promises we've made to our future.

The City's 2021 Sustainability Action Plan represents Spokane's aspirations for a future of clean & renweable energy powering our lives. The plan cuts across numuors sectors, and while focusing on measurable reductions in greenhouse gas emissions, it also sets priorities for building resilient neighborhoods by investing in our local communities and economy with healthy ecosystems, economic opportunity, equity, and health & wellbeing remaining central.
